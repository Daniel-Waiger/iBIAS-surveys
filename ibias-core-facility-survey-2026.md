# iBIAS Core Facility Community Survey 2026 (Google Forms Build Sheet)

## Form Title
iBIAS 2026 Core Facility Community Survey: Shape the Coming Year

## Form Description
The Israeli Bio-Image Analysis Society (iBIAS) is planning next year's activities for core facilities. Your feedback will directly shape workshops, training content, and community events. Please complete this short survey (about 3-5 minutes).

---

### Q1
Question: What is your main focus area in your core facility?
Type: Multiple choice
Required: Yes
Options:
- Microscopy support and imaging pipelines
- Quantitative image analysis support
- Training and user onboarding
- AI and deep learning analysis support
- Data management and reproducibility
- Workflow design and automation
- Other (please specify)
